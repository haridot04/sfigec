About

കഴിഞ്ഞ രണ്ട് പതിറ്റാണ്ടായി GECയുടെ രാഷ്ട്രീയ മുഖമാണ് SFI. GEC ക്ക് എക്കാലത്തും വ്യക്തമായ നിലപാടുണ്ട്. അത് അടിച്ചമർത്തപ്പെട്ടവരുടെ ശബ്ദമായ SFIയുടെ നിലപാടാണ്.അത് കഴിഞ്ഞ രണ്ട് ദശകങ്ങളായി കോളേജ് യൂണിയൻ ഇലക്ഷനിൽ GECയുടെ ഭരണസാരഥ്യം വലിയ ഭൂരിപക്ഷത്തിൽ SFI യെ ഏൽപ്പിച്ചു തന്നുകൊണ്ടിരിക്കുന്നു .GECയിൽ ജാതിയുടെയോ മതത്തിന്റെയോ ലിംഗത്തിന്റെയോ അതിർരേഖകളില്ല. പുരുഷ മേൽക്കോയ്മ നിറഞ്ഞ സമൂഹം പെണ്ണിനെ അളക്കുന്ന അളവുകോലുകൾ ഇല്ല ഇവിടെ. രാത്രികൾ പെണ്ണിന്റെ കൂടിയാണെന്ന് ഉറക്കെ പ്രഖ്യാപിച്ചു കൊണ്ട് ലേഡീസ് ഹോസ്റ്റലിലെ സമയക്രമം മെൻസ് ഹോസ്റ്റലിലേതിന് സമാനമാക്കണം എന്നാവശ്യപ്പെട്ടു സമരങ്ങൾ നടന്ന ഇടമാണിവിടം. 20 വർഷങ്ങൾക്കു മുമ്പ് ആണെന്നോ പെണ്ണെന്നോ വ്യത്യാസമില്ലാതെ റാഗിങ് കാരണം സ്വൈര്യമായി നടക്കാൻ ഭയന്നിരുന്ന വിദ്യാർത്ഥികളുള്ള കോളേജിൽ നിന്നും പ്രായത്തിന്റെ അതിർരേഖകളില്ലാതെ സൗഹൃദം പൂക്കുന്നിടമായി GECയെ മാറ്റിയത് ഞങ്ങളുടെ പൂർവികന്മാരുടെ മാനവികതയുടെ രാഷ്ട്രീയമായിരുന്നു.

Unit Initiatives

Kaithaang

Keeping in mind the challenges that first year students face in academics, SFI GEC launched a YouTube channel under the initiative of "Kaithaang". This aimed at providing video lectures for all subjects for first year students. With the latest revision of the KTU syllabus looming over them, notes for all subjects too were provided in an online platform for all to access. The timely lectures came as a huge relief for the students to catch up on missed classes and difficult topics, the highlight of the initiative being that senior students were the ones tutoring them.

Padheyam

Commemorating the death of a young man named Madhu, who was the victim of an inhumane mass trial for alleged theft, SFI GEC Unit continues it's noble initiative of providing meals for the not-so-fortunate sections of our society, on the 22nd of every month, unabated even during this covid crisis. The price he had to pay for his insatiable hunger was his life itself. Providing for the marginalised , even as little as we could provide, is something which we take huge pride in, as we set out on the 22nd of each month to prove that humanity still exists.

WOW GEC

Women of Wisdom (WOW) is a platform that empowers the women of GEC and encourage them to step forward with their ideas. This is organised by Mathrukam GEC unit in collaboration with the college union. WOW GEC addresses the issues faced by women and the gender issues that still prevail in the society. With various initiatives and events, this platform has always raised its strong voice against the heinous activities and problems that exist around us. WOW GEC has voiced the experiences of countless GECians, and with ventures like the 'No-Go zone' ( Sex Education series) they have broken the walls of ignorance and stereotypes that surround us.

Blood Donation

What more can be said about the importance of donating blood that hasn't yet been said! Our college has been the go-to spot for all hospitals and blood banks in and around Thrissur, as each student here values the importance of giving blood and saving lives. Our people here are ready to be called upon at any time, be it day or night. After all, blood is meant to be circulated, pass it on!

GEC Commune

Sitting at home and doing nothing during this lockdown period? SFI GEC had the solution to that too! After all we engineers are meant to be working round the clock, so sitting idle is not really our cup of tea. With this in mind the initiative of GEC Commune was launched, in a bid to provide students with an opportunity to water their passion by working from home in various fields and earning a few bucks along the way too! A team of experts were summoned for each field during an initial training period, after which freelance opportunities were created for those who enrolled, in fields like graphic designing, content writing, web designing, digital marketing, architectural design and many many more.
